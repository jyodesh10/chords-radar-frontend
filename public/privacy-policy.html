Privacy Policy for Chords Radar Nepal

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Chords Radar Nepal ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you provide directly and information collected automatically.

  • Account Information: When you log in (e.g., via Google), we receive your basic profile info such as your name, email address, and profile picture to personalize your experience.
  • User Content: We store the audio recordings and song covers you choose to record and post within the App.
  • Automated Data (AdMob): We use Google AdMob, which collects IP addresses, Advertising IDs (AAID/IDFA), and device performance logs to serve personalized ads.

3. Use of Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Enable you to post and share your musical recordings.
  • Improve App performance and fix technical bugs.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data (Profile and Audio Recordings) is stored securely using cloud services (such as Firebase or Supabase). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your info, but no method of digital storage is 100% secure.

5. GDPR & International Users

For users in the EEA, we request explicit consent for personalized ads via a Consent Management Provider. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To request data deletion, please contact us at the email below.

6. Children’s Privacy

Chords Radar Nepal does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our features or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a new "Effective Date."
